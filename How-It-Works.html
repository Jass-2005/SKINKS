How Skinks Works

Connecting local needs with local skills in just a few simple steps.

For Hirers (I need help!)

1. Post Your Task

Describe what you need, set your budget (fixed or hourly), and define the location (in-person or virtual). It's free to post!

2. Find Local Talent

Skinks instantly notifies verified talent within your 5km radius. You can also browse profiles and invite skilled neighbors to apply.

3. Hire & Pay Securely

Chat privately, compare offers, and hire the best person for your job. Once the task is done, pay securely through the platform.

For Talent (I have skills!)

1. Create Your Profile

Showcase your expertise, set your hourly rate or fixed prices, and define your local service area. Get verified to build trust.

2. Get Notified Locally

Receive real-time alerts for new tasks posted in your neighborhood (within 5km) that match your skills. No more searching!

3. Apply & Get Hired

Send offers for tasks you want to complete. Chat with the hirer, get hired, and earn money right in your community.
